# Gym Business Audit System

A complete B2B consulting product built for CrossFit and boutique fitness gym owners. Designed to diagnose revenue leaks, quantify the opportunity, and deliver a 90-day action plan. Built from scratch, deployed at Wodapalooza, and validated against real gym owner conversations.

---

## What this is

Every document here was designed to work as a system. The pre-call intake feeds the video call audit, which feeds the prescription report, which triggers the follow-up sequence. This is not a template or a course. It is a product.

---

## The system: 3 parts

### Part 1: Pre-Call Data Pack
A structured electronic intake the gym owner completes 48 hours before the call. Covers gym profile, revenue breakdown, cost structure, key metrics, and goals. Eliminates guesswork on the day and signals seriousness from both sides.

### Part 2: Video Call Audit (100-point diagnostic)
A 60-90 minute structured conversation across 4 business categories: Retention, Revenue (ARM), Acquisition, and Systems and Operations. Every question has a benchmark. Every answer moves a score.

Scoring: HIGH impact = 3 pts, MED impact = 2 pts, 4 categories x 25 pts = 100 pts total.

Score bands:
- 83-100: Strong
- 62-82: Good potential
- Below 62: Priority work

### Part 3: The Prescription
A written report with a personalised revenue gap estimate, score breakdown, and 90-day action plan calibrated to the gym's exact score profile.

---

## Revenue opportunity framework

Three gaps are calculated from the pre-call data and presented as the headline of every report:

| Gap | Formula | Example |
|-----|---------|---------|
| ARM gap | ($175 benchmark - actual ARM) x members | ($175 - $145) x 100 = $3,000/mo |
| Churn gap | (actual churn % - 3%) x members x ARM | (7% - 3%) x 100 x $150 = $600/mo |
| Conversion gap | (50% - actual conv %) x monthly trials x ARM | (50% - 30%) x 10 x $150 = $300/mo |
| Total opportunity | ARM gap + Churn gap + Conversion gap | $3,900/mo |

---

## KPI benchmarks

| KPI | Formula | Benchmark |
|-----|---------|-----------|
| ARM | MRR / members | > $175/month |
| Churn rate | Cancellations / members x 100 | < 5%/month |
| LTV | ARM x LEG | > $1,800 |
| LEG | Avg months to churn | > 12 months |
| Conversion rate | Paid trials / total trials x 100 | > 50% |
| Payroll % | Payroll / revenue x 100 | 35-45% |
| Cost per lead | Spend / leads per channel | < $50 paid / < $20 referral |

## The interactive audit tool

`GymQuickAudit.html` is a mobile-first lead capture quiz built for use at events like Wodapalooza. It was designed to qualify a gym owner as a potential client in under 3 minutes on a tablet.

Features:
- 5-question diagnostic scoring retention, conversion, revenue, experience, and systems
- Real-time revenue gap calculation based on member count and current ARM
- Lead capture (name, email, phone, gym name)
- Willingness-to-pay question
- Score-based result screen with personalised messaging across 3 tiers
- Hidden admin panel (tap the footer 5 times) with lead analytics, score distribution by domain, and CSV export
- Works fully offline as a downloaded file

## Follow-up system

`FollowUp_Templates.pdf` covers same-day follow-up messaging after event conversations, segmented by score:

- Low score (below 5): clear pain, priority outreach, audit offer
- Mid score (5-7): visible potential, warm follow-up, 20-min call offer
- High score (7+): long-term relationship, strategic framing
- SMS template for anyone who gave their number

The golden rule: personalise one thing per message (first name + one specific detail from the conversation). Send within 2 hours of the conversation.
