🪣 Three-Bucket Withdrawal Strategy
At retirement, restructure your portfolio into three buckets. This protects you from being forced to sell growth assets during a market downturn.
How withdrawals work: Take income from Bucket 1. When it runs low, refill from Bucket 2 dividends. Every 2-3 years in good market years, rebalance Bucket 3 into Bucket 2. In bad years — leave Bucket 3 alone.

🗓️ Social Security Timing
Every year you wait past 62 increases your monthly benefit. The difference between claiming at 62 vs 70 can be 75%+ more per month — for life.
The break-even point is typically around age 78-80. If you expect to live past 80, waiting to claim is usually the better mathematical choice — especially for women, who statistically live longer.
